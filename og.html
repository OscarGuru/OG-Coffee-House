About OG Coffee House

OG Coffee House brings you the world’s finest coffee beans, sourced from historic estates and roasted to perfection. Our journey began with a simple mission: to make premium, freshly roasted coffee accessible to everyone, every day.

Honoring the centuries-old tradition of coffee craftsmanship, OG Coffee House carries forward the legacy of OG coffee beans from their historic origins to your modern table.

Message from the Founder:
"We believe every cup of coffee should be a memorable experience. Thank you for being part of our journey!"

Our Journey

  • 2022 OG Coffee House founded in Ludhiana, Punjab.
  • 2023 Launched our first signature blend.
  • 2024 Expanded to online sales and nationwide delivery.
  • 2025 Trusted by thousands of coffee lovers across the globe.
